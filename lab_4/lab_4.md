## Exercitiul 7

#### Enunt:
7. Puterea unui semnal este $P_{semnal} = 90dB$. Se cunoaste raportul semnal-zgomot, $SNR_{dB} = 80 dB$. Care este puterea zgomotului?

$$
\begin{aligned}
SNR =& \frac{P_{semnal}}{P_{zgomot}} \\
SNR_{dB} =& 10 \log _{10}{SNR}
\\
80_{dB} =& 10 \log _{10}{\frac{P_{semnal}}{P_{zgomot}}}
\\
80_{dB} =&  10\log _{10}{P_{semnal}} - 10\log _{10}{P_{zgomot}}
\\
=> 80_{dB} =&  90P_{semnal}[db] - 10\log _{10}{P_{zgomot}} \\
=> 80_{dB} =&  90P_{semnal}[db] -P_{zgomot}[dB]
\\
=> P_{zgomot}[dB] =& 10 [dB]

\end{aligned}
$$
